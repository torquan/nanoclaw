### Progression Rules
- Hit all reps across all sets → add 2.5kg next session
- Miss reps on last set only → keep weight, aim for full reps
- Miss reps on 2+ sets → drop 2.5kg, rebuild
- Log RPE to track fatigue

## Miley — Glute Growth Plan (2×/week gym + 1× bouldering/dancing)

**Schedule:** Day A and Day B with 2-3 days between. Bouldering/dancing on weekends.

### Day A — Glute + Hamstring (~55 min)

| # | Exercise | Sets × Reps | Start Weight | Notes |
|---|----------|-------------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | Hip Thrust | 3 × 8-10 | 135kg | The king of glute exercises |
| 2 | Romanian Deadlift (RDL) | 3 × 10 | 50kg | Replaces conventional deadlift — better for hypertrophy |
| 3 | Bulgarian Split Squat | 3 × 10/leg | 12kg DBs | Massive glute stretch |
| 4 | Lying Leg Curl | 2 × 10 | 38.75kg | Hamstring isolation |
| 5 | Cable Kickback | 2 × 15/leg | 10kg | Isolation finisher |
| + | Stretching (splits work) | | | 10 min |

### Day B — Glute + Quad (~55 min)

| # | Exercise | Sets × Reps | Start Weight | Notes |
|---|----------|-------------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | Leg Press (feet high+wide) | 3 × 8 | 170kg | Pyramid: 170→178→185 |
| 2 | Hip Thrust | 3 × 8-10 | 135kg | 2×/week frequency |
| 3 | RDL | 3 × 10 | 50kg | Double duty: glutes + hamstrings |
| 4 | Hip Adduction | 2 × 12 | 50kg | Glute medius — side roundness |
| 5 | Back Extension | 2 × 10 | 25kg | Posterior chain finisher |
| + | Stretching (splits work) | | | 10 min |

### 9-Week Progression (Apr 12 – Jun 12)

**Weeks 1-3:** Build base — focus on form (especially RDL), add 2.5kg/week to hip thrust + leg press
**Weeks 4-6:** Push intensity — hip thrust target 150kg, leg press 195-200kg, add drop sets
**Weeks 7-9:** Peak + test — test new 8RM on goal lifts, deload final week if needed

### Key Principles
- 18 direct glute sets/week (optimal: 16-20 for experienced)
- Hip thrust 2×/week for max glute frequency
- RDL over conventional deadlift for more glute stretch under load
- No squats, no abductors (per Miley's preference)
